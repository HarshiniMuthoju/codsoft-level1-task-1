Paradise - It's a World famous Biryani

Paradise Biryani is a renowned restaurant chain in Hyderabad, India, famous for its delectable and flavourful biryani dishes, including the Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, served with succulent meat or aromatic spices, and often considered one of the city's culinary treasures.
